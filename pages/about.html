Who is Bujin?

BUJIN is the patronymic name of Amir Harith bin Mat Kashim, a young batik maker who has made a name for himself in the country and abroad. He has won various batik and art competitions and was honored as the country's Best Young Handicraft Entrepreneur in 2018.

Establishment

The Bujins Batik business was started by Amir Harith bin Mat Kashim after he completed his Batik Craft Art Diploma at the National Craft Institute, Rawang, Selangor, in 2011. It was officially registered by Fakrul Azlee bin Fakurod Rodzi and Farhi bin Zainal under the name Bujins L Batik Sdn. Bhd. In 2017, the company was officially taken over by Taufiq bin Hussien.

Academy

In 2016, the team from Bujins Academy won first prize in the handcraft category, second place in soft furnishing, and third place in the fashion category at The Endon Seri Cup Batik Competition, which was previously won by Bujin himself in 2012. They also triumphed in the Inaugural Kelantan Batik Challenge 2016, organized by the Foundation Noor Al Syakur.
